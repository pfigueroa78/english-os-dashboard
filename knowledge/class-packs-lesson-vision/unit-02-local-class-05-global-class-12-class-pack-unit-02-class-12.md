           by the TV show Solving Mysteries. Choose the ones discovered to be true.
           D 1. Using a cell phone can cause a fire at a gas station.

           D 3. A person can break a glass using just his or her voice.

           D 4. Yawning is contagious.

           2. Why did Adam doubt that talking to plants could help them grow?
