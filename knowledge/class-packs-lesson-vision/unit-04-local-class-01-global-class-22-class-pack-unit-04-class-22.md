             Reduced time clauses

             Notice how these clauses show time relationships. If the subject is the same in both clauses of
             the sentence, time clauses with (right) before, (right) after, and while can be reduced.
             After I finish I After finishing my workout, I head to the office.
                   I

             I'm not very approachable right after I wake up I right after waking up!
             However, other time clauses cannot usually be reduced.
             Ever since I was a kid, I've had trouble getting up early.
             As soon as I get up in the morning, I race off to the gym.
             Until I've had my coffee, I'm such a grouch.
             Whenever you have to work with numbers, plan to do it around noon.
