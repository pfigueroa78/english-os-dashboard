               find out what Dave Caroll did. Did he take any of the steps you discussed?

               are not part of Dave's story. Then take turns telling the story in your own words.

                  a. Dave decided to use his songwriting skills to address his problem in a creative way.
                  b. Dave rejected the airline's offer even though they'd doubled his original request.
                  c. Dave sued the airline for the amount of money it would cost to repair his guitar.
                  d. Dave's guitar was damaged by an airline, and they refused to compensate him.

               __ e. The airline offered Dave what he wanted after he posted his song on YouTube.
                  f. Dave gave up on his dreams of becoming a musician after 20 years in the business.
               __ g. Dave helped start an online consumer group and relaunched his career.

             C Group work Discuss these questions. Then share your answers with the class.
               1. What made Dave finally feel empowered during his struggle with the airline?
